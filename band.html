Identity band

This band acts as a ‘identity’ band for each child below 5 years old. Their identity and health information such as parents name, contact number or medical history, allergies are stored in this band.The authorized personnel can access the information by scanning the band through RFID technology.

×

Health Tracker

Able to monitor children health by heartrate, movement and body temperature with aid of artificial intelligence technology.

×

Safety Tracker

Can track the location of the children by using Global Positioning System (GPS). Besides, the 180° camera lens will also able to take start recording in order to protect the children if any incident happens.

×

Food Analysis

Can calculate the nutrition and calories of the food taking in by using our 180° camera lens with AI technology with the corporation with Google Lens. MyBand will also give recommendation of which type of food needed by the children.

×

Child Reminder

Act as a reminder band. After every consultation, the doctor will transfer the advice for the child to the band in order to remind the parents of the child to complete the task ordered by the doctors.

×

Education Analysis

Can sync with learning device. With aid of wireless signals, it is able to detect the emotion of the child by analyzing camera lens to know what they exposed to and also heartrate. By using MyBand, the children’s field of interest and their talent will be able to discovered.

Cost of components

Considering the advanced functions and the features of the band, it may seem unrealistic to make MyBand components with a lower cost. However, the currently most advanced commercial smartwatch, Apple Watch, only cost $83.70 to manufacture. Taking the cost of the fancy display and a relatively powerful processor which made up 36% of the total cost into account, the cost of the band can be greatly reduced by replacing the display and processor that cost $20.50 and $10.20 respectively with cheaper alternatives. Therefore, we expect the cost of the band to be in the range of $30-40.)

×

Lowering the price

Since our product MyBand is mainly will be cooperated with the government of the country, by mass-production, the economies of scale can be achieved. Furthermore, the economies of scale includes lowering the cost when the company purchases the components on bulk and increased efficiency when the company employ specialized labour and machinery. Hence, the overall efficiency and price of MyBand will be reduced to a price that affordable by the publics.

How we make it possible

We make it possible by connecting all existing databases. As for databases, government and authorities shall upgrade their existing database to be connected to each other. This will allow real-time data sharing and improved communication among hospitals,clinics and other related authorities. By doing so, there is no need for a huge investment for building a whole new system to achieve the proposed solution. Other than that, the network technology in our country, Malaysia is matured enough to support the fast data transmission. Therefore, MyBand is possible to achieve.
